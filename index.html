About the Hackathon

FABRIQUATHON 2K26 is a national-level 24-hour hands-on hardware hackathon where engineering minds from across India build real, working prototypes to solve real-world problems through two competitive tracks.

What Makes It Different

This is a build-first hackathon designed for serious makers. Teams ideate, pitch, fabricate, test, and present under one roof with continuous mentoring support.

  • National level participation
  • Hardware-centric prototype outcomes
  • 24-hour rapid engineering cycle
  • Cross-domain team collaboration

Rules & Eligibility

Eligibility

  • Open to UG/PG Students (Engineering / Technology / Science)
  • Teams of maximum 3 members
  • Interdisciplinary teams are encouraged

Team Formation

  • Nominate one Team Leader
  • Register before the deadline
  • Choose any one of the 2 available Domains/Tracks

Rules & Conduct

  • Original work only - no plagiarism
  • Bring your own hardware components
  • Follow all campus rules
  • Misconduct or plagiarism leads to disqualification
